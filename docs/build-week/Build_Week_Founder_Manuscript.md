Purpose: Capture the approved founder narrative developed during the Build Week preparation session. This document is intended to be expanded and refined into the final submission.

## The Beginning

There has to be an easier way to zero a scope.

That simple question became the spark that eventually grew into SCZN3. What began as a conversation about making zeroing easier became a journey of asking better questions and following the evidence wherever it led.

## The Tomato

One of the advantages I had was that I didn't come from the software industry.

I spent my career in retail with a grocery chain, where I learned that simple systems can carry extraordinary amounts of information.

One day, I was looking at a tomato.

Like millions of other people, I had seen the little PLU (Price Look-Up) sticker thousands of times. Most shoppers never think twice about it. It's just a number on a piece of produce.

But I knew what that number represented.

It wasn't just a price.

It was an identity.

That small code connected the tomato to an entire retail system. The register instantly knew what it was, how to price it, how to track it, and everything the store needed to know. The customer only saw a sticker. The store saw information.

Then a question hit me.

Why couldn't a shooting target work the same way?

What if every target carried its own identity?

What if scanning a code on the target instantly connected the shooter to everything the platform knew about that specific target?

At that moment, I stopped thinking about QR codes as something you scanned. I started thinking about them as the digital identity of a physical object.

The paper target wouldn't become smarter.

The connection between the paper and the platform would.

That realization became one of the foundational ideas behind SCZN3.

## What Is SCZN3?

SCZN3 is an Explainable After-Shot Intelligence Platform.

Its purpose is to transform a photograph of a shooting target into information the shooter can immediately understand and use.

Every supported target has a governed identity.
Every measurement is based on known geometry.
Every conclusion is explainable.
Every result can be traced back to the evidence that produced it.

SCZN3 doesn't change the target.

It changes what the target is able to tell the shooter.

## Why It Matters

SCZN3 was never intended to replace existing shooting organizations. It was designed to work alongside them, preserving their rules while giving shooters a clearer understanding of their own performance.

The goal was never to change shooting.

The goal was to make every shot more understandable.

## One Memorable Step

SCZN3 wasn't built in one giant leap. It was built one memorable step at a time. One question led to one discovery. One discovery led to another. With the help of AI, those small steps became a platform.

My hope is that the next person with a simple question realizes they don't have to know the entire path. Sometimes all you need is the courage to take one memorable step.
